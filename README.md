## 🎲 What is Super Tic-Tac-Toe?

**Super Tic-Tac-Toe** is an advanced version of the classic Tic-Tac-Toe game played on a **9×9 grid**. The grid is divided into **9 smaller 3×3 sub-grids**, making a total of **81 spaces**!

### 🏆 How to Win
- **Win a sub-grid:** Get three of your marks (X or O) in a row within a small 3×3 grid — just like regular Tic-Tac-Toe.
- **Win the game:** Win **three sub-grids in a row** on the main board (horizontally, vertically, or diagonally).

### 📜 How to Play

1. **First Move:** Player 1 (X) can place their mark **anywhere** on the board.
2. **The Twist:** Where you place your mark determines **where your opponent must play next**. For example, if you place your mark in the **top-right corner** of a sub-grid, your opponent must play in the **top-right sub-grid** of the main board. 🔄
3. **Full/Won Sub-Grids:** If you're sent to a sub-grid that's already full or won, you get a **free move** — place your mark anywhere!
4. **Take Turns:** Players alternate placing X's and O's, following the directional rule.
5. **Victory:** The first player to control **three sub-grids in a row** wins the game! 🎉

> 💡 **Strategy Tip:** Think ahead! Every move not only affects the small grid you're playing in but also dictates where your opponent will play next. It's chess disguised as Tic-Tac-Toe. ♟️

## 🚀 How to Open & Play the Game

It's super easy — **even a kid can do it!** 😄

### Step-by-Step Guide:

1. **Open the folder** where this game is located on your computer. 📂
2. Navigate to:
   ```
   Supertictactoe By Raka\bin\Debug\net8.0-windows\
   ```
3. Find the file named:
   ```
   Supertictactoe Trial 1.exe
   ```
4. **Double-click it!** 🖱️✨
5. The game will open and you're ready to play! 🎮

> **That's it!** No installation, no complicated setup. Just click and play! 🎉
